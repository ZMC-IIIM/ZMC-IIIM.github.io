Biography

Hello, I’m Zhiming Cheng, an Associate professor at the School of Communication Engineering, Hangzhou Dianzi University. I received my Ph.D. degree in Control science and Engineering from the Hangzhou Dianzi University under the supervision of Prof. Chenggang Yan in 2024.

My research mainly focuses on object detection and their applications, including spherical image object detection, remote sensing image object detection and bev object detection.
